Open Hexagon

Arcade Fast-Paced Open Source Geometric Rhythm

About Open Hexagon

Open Hexagon is an intense, fast-paced arcade game that will test your reflexes and concentration. Navigate a small triangle around the inside of a hexagon as walls close in from all sides. The goal is simple: survive as long as possible while the speed increases and patterns become more complex.

Created by Vittorio Romeo, this open-source game features mesmerizing geometric patterns, pulsing electronic music, and increasingly challenging levels that will keep you on the edge of your seat. The minimalist design and fluid gameplay create an almost hypnotic experience.

With its perfect blend of simplicity and challenge, Open Hexagon has become a favorite among arcade game enthusiasts and speedrunners alike. Can you master the hexagon and achieve the highest score?

Basic Controls

  • Left Arrow / A: Move counter-clockwise around the hexagon
  • Right Arrow / D: Move clockwise around the hexagon
  • Space: Pause the game
  • R: Restart the current level
  • ESC: Return to main menu

Game Objective

  • Control the small triangle in the center of the hexagon
  • Avoid touching the walls that spiral inward
  • Survive as long as possible to achieve a high score
  • The game gets faster and more complex over time
  • Look for gaps in the walls to stay alive

Pro Tips

  • Stay calm and focus on the rhythm of the music
  • Look ahead to anticipate wall patterns
  • Practice makes perfect - each level has unique patterns
  • Don't panic when the speed increases
  • Learn to recognize safe zones and dangerous patterns
