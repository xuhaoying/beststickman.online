About Stickman Games

Stickman games have become one of the most popular casual gaming genres worldwide. With their simple yet captivating graphics and addictive gameplay, these games provide hours of entertainment for players of all ages.

Count Masters: Stickman Games takes the genre to a new level with its innovative approach to stickman-based action. In this game, you control an army of stickmen that grows or shrinks based on your strategic decisions and reflexes.

The objective is simple: build your army by collecting as many stickmen as possible while navigating through various obstacles and defeating enemies along the way. The more stickmen you have, the stronger your force becomes.

With its intuitive controls and escalating difficulty, Count Masters offers a perfect balance of casual fun and challenging gameplay that keeps players coming back for more.

How to Play Count Masters

  1. Controls: Use mouse or touchscreen to move your stickman army left and right across the path.
  2. Building Your Army: Run through gates with "+" signs to multiply your stickmen or numbers to add that specific amount.
  3. Avoiding Losses: Be careful of gates with "-" signs or division symbols as they will reduce your army size.
  4. Battles: When you encounter enemy stickmen, your armies will automatically fight. The larger army usually wins.
  5. Boss Fights: At the end of levels, you'll face bosses. Circle around them while attacking to defeat them.
